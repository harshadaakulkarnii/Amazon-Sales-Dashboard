3. Product Performance - Product table, treemap, category matrix
4. Customer & Operations - Geographic analysis, gauges, order status

## Key Business Insights
- US dominates revenue at $64M (70% of total)
- 74.63% delivery success rate
- Electronics highest revenue category at ~$15.6M
- Credit Card most popular payment method (35%)
- 3.03% cancellation rate (healthy for e-commerce)
